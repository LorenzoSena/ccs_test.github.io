Useful traits for the description of rice cultivars

There are many traits to evaluate in order to describe rice plants and divide them in different cultivars. Some of the most important are:

  • Plant height
  • Time of flowering
  • Shape of the seed
  • Presence of node pigmentation

You can find the list of all the standard traits for the description of rice cultivars at the CPVO (Community Plant Variety Office)
